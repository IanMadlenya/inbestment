Understanding Your Goals

The results are ordered by priority (clockwise starting from the "Checking" semi-circle graph).


Results are calculated as:
1. Checking - one month worth of living expenses
2. Savings - three months worth of living expenses
3. 401K match - (if applicable), calculated based on user inputted percent match and percent of salary to match
4. IRA - maximum $5,500 IRS contribution
5. 401K - (if applicable), maximum $18,000 IRS contribution less 401K match
6. Brokerage - remaining assets


"Target Remaining" in black denotes the amount that the user should be funding that account.
"Amount Funded" in blue denotes the amount that the user is able to fund that account based on their assets.
"Not Applicable" in grey is reserved for accounts that do not apply to the user.
Note that the brokerage account is either "Not Applicable" if the user does not have enough remaining assets or 100% "Amount Funded", since there is no target value.
